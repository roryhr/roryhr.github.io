Bio

For my master’s thesis I designed a fluid dynamics experiment that measured the state of the fluid using a novel computer vision system. Similarly, my bachelor’s work pioneered a measurement technique that collected data from splashes from two high-speed cameras. Lacking a theory to draw conclusions from my data, I developed a new theory for my analysis.

I'm currently fascinated by deep convolutional networks, Kaggle, and Reinforcement Learning. Applications for RL include autonomous driving, autonomous trading, autonomous...you name it!
